Meet Grandmaster Tom

As a 7th Dan Black Belt, he has devoted his life to helping students build confidence, discipline, and strength that carries far beyond the mat.

My goal has always been bigger than Taekwondo itself. I want every student to walk into Pegasus feeling supported, valued, and capable of achieving greatness. One day, I hope my students look back and remember the confidence, discipline, memories, and family we built together.

To us, Master Tom is far more than an instructor — he is a mentor, role model, and father figure who genuinely cares about every single student. His values, dedication, and warmth are what make Pegasus Taekwondo so special.

What we offer

Training programmes
for every age

Ages 3–8

Young Warriors

Fun, energetic, and structured classes that build confidence, focus, coordination, discipline, and respect in a safe and supportive environment where young students can thrive.

Ages 9–13

Intermediate

Progressive and challenging training designed to develop confidence, resilience, leadership, strong technique, and controlled sparring within a motivating team environment.

Ages 14+

Advanced / Adults

Taekwondo training focused on patterns, sparring, self-defence, fitness, discipline, and personal growth. Whether starting fresh or continuing the journey, students are pushed to become stronger on and off the mat.

Pegasus Taekwondo is proud to be an inclusive club. We welcome students with autism and special needs — our instructors are experienced in creating a supportive, patient, and structured environment for every learner.
