Watch & learn

Patterns in motion

Taekwondo patterns (Tul) are sequences of movements practised to develop technique, balance, and discipline. Watch our students demonstrate the first three ITF patterns.

Pattern No. 1
Chon-Ji
The first pattern — meaning "Heaven and Earth." The foundation of all ITF Taekwondo practice.
▶ Watch on YouTube
Pattern No. 2
Dan-Gun
Named after the legendary founder of Korea. Builds on Chon-Ji with greater complexity and range.
▶ Watch on YouTube
Pattern No. 3
Do-San
Named after patriot Ahn Chang-Ho. Introduces new techniques and directional movements.
